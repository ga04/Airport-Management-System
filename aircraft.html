Wide-Body Aircraft:

  • Boeing 777: This aircraft comes in different versions, including the 777-200, 777-300, and 777X. It can carry a large number of passengers over long distances.
  • Airbus A330: The A330 is available in various models, such as the A330-200 and A330-300, and is often used for long-haul routes.
  • Boeing 787 Dreamliner: Known for its fuel efficiency and passenger comfort, the Dreamliner is available in the 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10 variants.

Narrow-Body Aircraft:

  • Boeing 737: The Boeing 737 is one of the most widely used narrow-body aircraft, with several models like the 737-700, 737-800, and 737 MAX series.
  • Airbus A320: The A320 family includes the A318, A319, A320, and A321, all of which are commonly used for short to medium-haul flights.

Business Jets:

  • Gulfstream G650: Known for its long-range capabilities and luxurious interiors.
  • Cessna Citation: A series of business jets designed for various missions, from short hops to long-distance travel.
  • Bombardier Challenger: A popular choice for business aviation, available in various models.
